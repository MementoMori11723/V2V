About Us

Echo Flow is an innovative platform designed to make communication easier through seamless voice-to-voice interactions. Our mission is to enable users to communicate naturally, without the need for complicated tools or interfaces. Echo Flow utilizes advanced speech processing technology to accurately capture and respond to user input, ensuring an immersive experience across various scenarios.
