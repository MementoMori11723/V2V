Privacy Policy

At Echo Flow, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. We understand the importance of keeping your data confidential, and we take this responsibility very seriously. Importantly, we do not store any of your data on our servers. All voice inputs and interactions are directly sent to our custom GPT model for processing in real-time, without being recorded or stored. This means that your conversations remain entirely confidential and ephemeral. We utilize robust data encryption protocols during transmission to safeguard your information from unauthorized access. Our infrastructure is designed with privacy in mind, ensuring that your voice data is processed securely and then immediately discarded. We are dedicated to providing you with a secure platform where you can communicate freely, knowing that your privacy is fully protected and that your data is never stored or shared.
